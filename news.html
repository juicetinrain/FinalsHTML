Educating the Future: SheppyToys Hosts Toy History Workshop

Published: December 5, 2024

SheppyToys recently organized a Toy History Workshop for local students in partnership with Toyville Elementary. The event featured an interactive presentation on the evolution of toys, hands-on vintage toy displays, and storytelling sessions. It was a fun and educational experience that inspired young minds to explore toy collecting and history.
