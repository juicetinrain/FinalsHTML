About Us

SheppyToys Collectibles is proud to be one of the most trusted local retailers of authentic anime figures in the Philippines. Recognized by passionate collectors and anime fans alike, we are dedicated to delivering a top-tier collectible experience that brings your favorite characters to life.

Our journey began with a dream to prove that Filipino collectors deserve easy access to genuine, high-quality anime merchandise—without the need to import or risk counterfeits. We built SheppyToys Collectibles not only to offer exciting figures from the world’s most beloved franchises, but to create a space where anime culture can thrive and be celebrated.

Over the years, we’ve grown from a humble online seller into a go-to destination for enthusiasts across the country, consistently blending affordability, authenticity, and fan-driven service. At SheppyToys, every figure tells a story—and every purchase supports a community built on passion, trust, and shared love for anime.
